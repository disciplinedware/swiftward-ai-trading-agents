Simulated PnL - How It Works

On-chain trade intents carry no execution price, so PnL is reconstructed from market data. For each approved trade we take the VWAP of the 1-minute Kraken candle at or after the intent timestamp (execution happens after the on-chain intent) and apply a 0.26% taker fee (Kraken lowest tier). Positions are tracked with FIFO lot accounting. This is the best approximation possible from on-chain data alone, but it is not actual exchange PnL. A few things to keep in mind: buy-and-hold of a single volatile asset can show large PnL from market movement, not strategy; some agents short-sell assets they don't hold (tracked as synthetic short positions); unrealized PnL is marked to the last available candle; trades without a matching on-chain approval are excluded (shown as UNKNOWN).
